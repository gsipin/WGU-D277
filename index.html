About Washington State 1

  • Official name is "State of Washington"
  • Named after George Washington
  • Became the 42nd State on November 11, 1889

Fun Facts 1

  1. State Flower: Pacific rhododendron
  2. State bird: American Goldfinch
  3. Population: 7.786 million (2022)
